TFT Applications

TFT has been used around the world to help people find relief from psychological and physical distress.

Applications:  

Seeing the successful implementation of TFT, helping communities heal from large-scale trauma, Dr. Caroline Sakai has been committed to teaching this self-treatment to help people enhance resilience, recover from trauma, reduce anxieties, treat phobias, and numerous other psychological and physical issues.

For the past 10 years she has been supporting trauma relief efforts in Rwanda to help with reconciliation post-genocide.

Many after surviving a traumatic experience had all but given up hope of leading a normal life, but as they began to heal, they had in common, a strong desire to help others.

Knowing that we can make a difference and that we each possess this capacity can help us to feel less overwhelmed and more empowered. And that can start us on path of recovery, healing and post-traumatic growth. If you would like to learn TFT or are interested in supporting trauma relief efforts.
